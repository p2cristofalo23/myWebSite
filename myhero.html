Dustin Johnson

Dustin Johnson currently is number one player in the world. His rookie year on the PGA Tour since 2008. During his Rookie season, he won his his first tournament at the Turning Stone Resort Championship. Since his win in 20008, he has racked up a total of 21 additional PGA Tour wins, and the winner of the 2016 Us Open Championship.

The restart of the 2020 PGA Tour season started off rough for Dustin Johnson. He had to with draw from two tournaments due to the inadequate performance by himself. Then he does this: 

 
T12th St. Jude Invitational 

T2nd PGA Championship

1st Northern Trust

2nd BMW Championship

This dominant performance moved him from number 6 in the world to number 1 in the world. He is also the leader in The Fedex Cup Playoffs, as they go into the final tournament, The Tour Championship.
